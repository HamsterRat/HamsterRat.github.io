NAMI Charlotte

NAMI Charlotte supports people affected by mental health conditions in Mecklenburg County through advocacy, education, support groups, and community programs. They help families and individuals navigate mental health resources and provide connection and guidance.

Visit NAMI Charlotte →

Santé Group – Mecklenburg County

The Santé Group provides 24/7 crisis intervention and behavioral health support. Their Mobile Crisis Team responds to mental health emergencies and substance use crises, helping individuals safely access care and community resources.

Mecklenburg County Emergency Contacts

This resource provides a comprehensive list of local emergency hotlines and crisis support numbers, including mental health, suicide prevention, domestic violence, and other urgent assistance lines for Mecklenburg County.

Mecklenburg County Health & Human Services (HHS)

Mecklenburg County HHS provides public health, social services, and community support programs, including health protection, family support, senior services, and access to financial and social assistance.
