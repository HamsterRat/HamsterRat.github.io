Habitat for Humanity Charlotte Region

Habitat Charlotte Region invites volunteers of all skill levels to help build affordable housing, assist in ReStore locations, or participate in youth and group programs. Training and guidance are provided for each role.

Mecklenburg County Parks & Recreation

Mecklenburg County Parks & Recreation offers opportunities to maintain parks, trails, and greenways. Roles include litter pickup, trail care, and facility support for individuals and groups looking to give back outdoors.

TreesCharlotte

TreesCharlotte relies on volunteers to plant and care for trees, pack seedlings, and assist with outreach events. Individuals, families, and groups can make a tangible impact on Charlotte’s urban forest and green spaces.

Volunteer with TreesCharlotte →

The Salvation Army of Greater Charlotte

The Salvation Army offers volunteers opportunities to serve meals, organize donation drives, assist youth programs, and help during seasonal initiatives. Both individuals and groups can get involved to support those facing hardship in the community.
